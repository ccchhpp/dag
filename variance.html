<<<<<<< HEAD GOALS

The aim of Variance is to create a series of paintings that can be viewed as-is as well as have a separate element of augmented reality and projection mapping.

These external alterations on the still image is a hybridization of printed media as well as interactive media and plays on the concept of viewing art through another channel. A viewer has the choice to observe the art piece undisturbed, or by pulling their AR app up on their phone.

The concept chosen for this piece coincides with the aspect of change that AR is able to accomplish, and reveals the unseen. More goals is to create more concrete concepts for the remaining pieces that are not necessarily exactly the same content wise, but similar thematically.
